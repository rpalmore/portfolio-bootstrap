About Me

Hi! I'm Rebecca. Thanks for checking out my portfolio. I've been a full-stack web developer since 2017, and a multimedia journalist and editor since 2002.

Fast-paced newsrooms unwittingly prepared me for computer science, where focus, intense attention to detail and quick problem-solving skills are essential, and a curious mind is what powers that need to look under the hood to try and answer a question that dogs so many of us: How does it work?

As a student of web development at Northwestern University, I’m continually learning how things work, from front-end design to server-side logic. Working with JavaScript and Node.js has been hugely challenging. But with each app or in-class activity, there is immense satisfaction. I’m excited to build on my skills.

As a digital editor at Chicago Tonight at WTTW-Channel 11, I manage a small, driven team of reporters, produce compelling stories for digital platforms, edit and program a news website and — as many in my field do at all hours of the day and night — obsess over metrics.

Here, you'll find class projects and homework from the 24-week full-stack web development program at Northwestern. I'll add new projects as I build them.
